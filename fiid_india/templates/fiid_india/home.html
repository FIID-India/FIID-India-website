Who we are

Fransalian Initiatives for Integral Development in India (FIID India) is a non-profit organization dedicated to promoting social welfare and economic development in rural communities across India. Our mission is to improve the lives of individuals in underserved areas by providing access to essential services, including healthcare, education, and vocational training.

What we aim to achieve

Our mission it promote social welfare and development, improving health and education, providing financial assistance to the needy, and establishing various institutions for the betterment of society. The organization aims to promote social, charitable, humanitarian, religious, and moral ideals, and bring harmony, equality, peace, and tranquility among people from all walks of life.
