What we aim to achieve

"Empowering Rural Communities for Sustainable Development: FIID India's Objectives"

  • Our aim is to uplift the living conditions of the community through various programs, seminars, workshops, and activities that promote social, charitable, humanitarian and philanthropic services. The organization aims to promote health and family welfare, including the prevention of drug addiction, by providing healthcare units, dispensaries, clinics, health-camps, de-addiction centers, and other allied activities to promote community healthcare and nutrition. They also seek to promote economic and social empowerment for women through microfinance and human resource development, among other programs.
  • We empower the indigent and make them self-reliant and attain economic development to improve the living standards of the people.
  • The organization intends to promote all-round development in society by establishing social activities, economic development programs in agriculture, environment, medical and educational spheres. They also seek to develop areas of mutual cooperation and understanding among different voluntary agencies, organizations, institutions, government agencies, groups, and individuals that are contributing to the welfare and development of the people.
  • The organization seeks to promote general welfare and physical, mental, material, spiritual, moral, and cultural advancement of people irrespective of their creed. We educate and train students, staff, social workers, and others in arts, science, culture, religion, philosophy, theology, educational skills, social action, etc.
  • This organization promotes the construction and running of schools, colleges, and non-formal educational institutions based on the ideals laid by great educationists of the country for the benefit of the public. We provide grants, scholarships, fellowships, and other forms of financial assistance to the needy and deserving students for pursuing education, vocational training, and skill development.
  • Finally, the organization aims to administer, organize, manage, encourage, associate, supervise, promote, and carry out programs for the establishment and/or maintenance of clinics, nursing homes, mobile clinics, vaccination and immunization centers, dispensaries, hospitals, rehabilitation centers, health clubs and counseling centers for the benefit of all irrespective of caste, creed, gender, or sect.
