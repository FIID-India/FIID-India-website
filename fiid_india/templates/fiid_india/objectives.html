What we aim to achieve

"Empowering Rural Communities for Sustainable Development: FIID India's Objectives"

  • The mission of this organization is to uplift the living conditions of the community through various programs, seminars, workshops, and activities that promote social, charitable, humanitarian, philanthropic, and religious services. The organization aims to promote health and family welfare, including the prevention of drug addiction, by providing healthcare units, dispensaries, clinics, health-camps, de-addiction centers, and other allied activities to promote community healthcare and nutrition. They also seek to promote economic and social empowerment for women through microfinance and human resource development, among other programs.
  • This organization aims to improve the living conditions of the poor and indigent by making people aware of the needs of the nation and developing a spirit of voluntary services and voluntarism. They also explore and develop local talents to generate self-reliance and cooperation among the people to attain economic development and to improve the living standards of the people.
  • The organization intends to promote all-round development in society by establishing social activities, economic development programs in agriculture, environment, medical and educational spheres. They also seek to develop areas of mutual cooperation and understanding among different voluntary agencies, organizations, institutions, government agencies, groups, and individuals that are contributing to the welfare and development of the people.
  • Additionally, the organization seeks to promote general welfare and physical, mental, material, spiritual, moral, and cultural advancement of people irrespective of their faith/religion. They aim to educate, train, and assist financially or otherwise, in education and training of students, teachers, staff, personnel of the Society, social workers, and others in arts, science, culture, religion, philosophy, theology, educational skills, social action, etc.
  • This organization promotes the construction and running of schools, colleges, and educational institutions based on the ideals laid by great educationists of the country for the benefit of the public. They provide grants, scholarships, fellowships, and other forms of financial assistance to needy and deserving students for pursuing education, vocational training, and skill development.
  • Finally, the organization aims to administer, organize, manage, encourage, associate, supervise, promote, and carry out programs for the establishment and/or maintenance of clinics, nursing homes, mobile clinics, vaccination and immunization centers, dispensaries, hospitals, rehabilitation centers, health clubs, and counseling centers, among others, for the benefit of all irrespective of caste, creed, gender, or sect.
