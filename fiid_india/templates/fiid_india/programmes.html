Programmes overview

At FIID India, we believe that our mission and vision can only be achieved through a holistic approach that addresses the root causes of poverty, inequality, and underdevelopment in rural communities. We undertake a wide range of activities to promote social welfare and economic development, including organizing various programs, seminars, activities, lectures, conferences, meetings, and workshops to raise awareness and promote active involvement in social, charitable, humanitarian, philanthropic, and religious services.

One of our main focuses is on promoting health and family welfare. We recognize that access to basic healthcare services is a fundamental right of every individual, and we strive to make quality healthcare services available to rural communities across India. To achieve this, we organize health camps, awareness programs, and training sessions on various health-related issues, including sanitation, hygiene, nutrition, and reproductive health.
